Introduction

For holding various tiles/components/anything else in situations like board games, I wanted a way of having things to hand to test.

Thus, I designed this to be able to quickly print to keep aside as my testers before commiting to a larger print. I embedded the numbers for the print into the bottom of the print itself so that after printing, I could easily remember what it is in a collection of these.

Everything is controlled by the parameters in the top marked section. File found here.
