Introduction

I went for a 3×5+3 keyboard in the Waterfowl R2. My previous keymap for my Charybdis was designed to allow me to transition to this by encouraging that I didn’t use the 4th row and 6th column. Even so, I had to move a few keys or make them combos, e.g. escape. There were also new keys that I had to add in, e.g. Bluetooth controls.
