Introduction

After getting my Charybdis from BastardKB a couple months ago, I’ve been going through various iterations of my keymap. I took the initial keymap that was provided, then modified it to have a Colemak-DH base and created a lot of the standard layers people have. I also took inspiration from a user called casuanoob’s layout with the brackets and some of the combo keys (and in a later post when I write it up, the gaming layer).

The keymap is also designed to aid me to have some duplication such that I would be able to use a 3×6 layout (if it has a pointing device) in the future.
