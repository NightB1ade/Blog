Introduction

Using my Elegoo Neptune 4 Pro, my first project was to print a insert for my Gaia Project board game. Using a combination of plans from Thingiverse (links below), the results are below.

Components

  • 1× Federation tokens and planet tray and lid
  • 3× Half-height, split trays – For 2× Power tokens and QICs, 1× Federation markers and skill usage tokens
  • 10× Lids for half-height, split trays and player boxes
  • 2× Map holders
  • 7× Player boxes
  • 6× Red player space stations
  • 1× Tile and card tray
