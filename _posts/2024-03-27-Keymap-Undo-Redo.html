Introduction

I started using the third thumb key on the right side as Ctrl-Z/Ctrl-Y for undo/redo and I have found that it’s weirdly useful.

I decided that I didn’t actually use the Magic2 key so have removed that from the keymap – but now contemplating what I should put there in its place.

I’ve nudged Num Lock to a thumb position as it’s not used often (and also prepare for a 3×5+3 that I’ve got on pre-order).
